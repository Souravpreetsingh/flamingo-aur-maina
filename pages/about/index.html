Our Story

A place to belong.

After spending nearly a decade in India's metro cities, our founders, Karanvir Singh Sapra and Rahul Yadav, returned to the mountains with a simple idea—to create the kind of place they would always want to come back to. A place where mornings begin with mountain views instead of alarms, where conversations need countless cups of tea, and where every guest is welcomed like an old friend.

Flamingo aur Maina (FAM) is a boutique café and stays nestled in the heart of Jibhi, surrounded by an apple orchard, 1.5 acres of open land, and uninterrupted 360° mountain views. Every corner of the property has been thoughtfully designed to slow you down—from our cozy wooden cabins and comfortable rooms to our café serving comforting food made with care.

Our Vision

A place to belong.

At FAM, we believe true hospitality isn't about grand lobbies or room service menus. It's about creating a space where people feel they truly belong. A place where the mountains feel closer, conversations flow more freely, and every moment is an invitation to slow down.

We are rooted in nature, powered by meaningful connections, and driven by a simple belief—that the best experiences are the ones we share. Whether it's a quiet morning in the apple orchard, an evening around the bonfire with new friends, or a cup of chai at our café, every detail at FAM is designed to turn a stay into a memory.

We are not building a hotel. We are building a community—one guest, one story, one welcome home at a time.
